How I work

Reliable systems over vague capability claims.

  • Build data and reporting systems that make ambiguous workflows measurable and usable.
  • Define metrics and quality checks so people can move faster without arguing over numbers.
  • Translate experimentation, analytics, and modeling outputs into concrete product or operational decisions.
  • Stay close to the ugly edge cases, because that is where trust is usually won or lost.
