What I studied

Transit access, safety, and connectivity around real hubs.

This project sits closer to planning and spatial analysis than to warehouse modeling, but it comes from the same instinct: understand how systems behave in the real world and make the trade-offs legible.

Why it matters

Good spatial analysis should make better planning decisions easier.

The StoryMap focuses on how transit hubs work for people, not just what the map looks like. The value is in connecting spatial context to better planning choices.
