What I studied

Transit access, safety, and connectivity around real hubs.

This project examines how transit hubs function in practice and how spatial context affects access, safety, and connectivity.

Why it matters

Good spatial analysis should support better planning decisions.

The StoryMap focuses on how transit hubs work for people, not just what a map can display. The value is in connecting spatial context to practical planning choices.
