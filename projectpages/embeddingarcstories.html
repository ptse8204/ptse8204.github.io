Why it belongs here

A domain project with the same systems lens.

This project sits closer to planning and spatial analysis than to warehouse modeling, but it belongs in the same portfolio because it reflects the same instinct: understand how systems behave in the real world and make the trade-offs legible.

What to look for

Access, connectivity, and decision context.

The StoryMap focuses on how transit hubs work for people, not just what the map looks like. The key value is in the connection between spatial context and better planning decisions.
