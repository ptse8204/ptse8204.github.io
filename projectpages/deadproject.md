<p data-reveal>
  These projects did not become long-term flagships, but each clarified something useful about feasibility, operating burden, or the gap between an interesting idea and a workable one.
</p>

<div class="archive-grid">
  <article class="archive-card" data-reveal>
    <div>
      <p class="archive-card__date">Winter 2018</p>
      <h3>Project Upcycle</h3>
    </div>
    <p>
      A fundraising concept for Enactus built around California CRV collection economics. The plan was financially plausible, but the operational burden was too high for a student organization to run cleanly.
    </p>
    <p class="archive-card__lesson"><strong>What carried forward:</strong> Feasible models still fail when labor intensity and execution overhead are underestimated.</p>
    <a class="text-link" href="{{ '/assets/ProjectUpcycle.pdf' | relative_url }}">Open artifact</a>
  </article>

  <article class="archive-card" data-reveal>
    <div>
      <p class="archive-card__date">Fall 2018</p>
      <h3>Solutions for beruby</h3>
    </div>
    <p>
      An early case competition proposing a subscription-style retention strategy for a cashback rewards company. The business model shift was ambitious, but it also required a stronger change in user behavior than the market probably supported.
    </p>
    <p class="archive-card__lesson"><strong>What carried forward:</strong> Strategy ideas need to be judged not just on elegance, but on how much user and market behavior they ask you to rewrite.</p>
    <a class="text-link" href="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1FrR5l_Tn3_pjkn-LJznG3mQnEn9_DHTagOAZq07nA3I/edit?usp=sharing">Open slides</a>
  </article>

  <article class="archive-card" data-reveal>
    <div>
      <p class="archive-card__date">2018 - 2020</p>
      <h3>Multiple QR-Code Verification Method</h3>
    </div>
    <p>
      A security concept for multi-step QR-based verification intended for lower-cost authentication flows. It never moved past the grant-seeking stage, but the idea came from a real concern about how weaker transaction environments handle identity and trust.
    </p>
    <p class="archive-card__lesson"><strong>What carried forward:</strong> Security ideas become more interesting when they can widen access rather than only raise technical sophistication.</p>
  </article>
</div>
